About SmartInvest

SmartInvest is a simple, beginner‑friendly web application that helps you figure out the best time to buy or sell stocks. It combines insider trading data and market insights to make complex financial information easier to understand.

Our Motive

Many new investors struggle to interpret market trends or find trustworthy signals. We built SmartInvest to bridge that gap and give everyone access to insights that professionals use, without needing years of experience.

How It Helps You

  • ✅ Clear buy/sell signals: instantly see if insiders are buying or selling a stock.
  • ✅ Beginner friendly: plain‑language explanations and an easy interface.
  • ✅ Save time: no need to dig through complex reports—everything is simplified for you.

The Benefits

With SmartInvest, you gain confidence in your investing decisions. You get data‑backed signals and helpful explanations, so you can make smarter moves in the market without feeling overwhelmed.
